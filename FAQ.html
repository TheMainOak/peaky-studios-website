Can I change things on my website after its made?

Yes, if you have revisions left on your package or at an extra fee if no revisions left.

How do I place an order for a website?

Click on the order button at the bottom of the package or simply email us with your requirements and we will get back to you with a quote.

How long does it take to deliver a full website?

That depends on the the requirements but we generally try to deploy websites within 3 months with the simpler ones deploying sooner.

What happens after the website has been up for 12 months?

You would then have to renew your subscription by either paying once off or monthly. The prices differ from month 13 onwards.

How long does it take until I get the logo?

We generally provide a client with 3 designs within 2 weeks. From there on out the client and our design team work hand in hand to fine tune a specific design until the desired outcome has been achieved.

What happens if I don't want to renew my subscription?

We provide you with the website files and say goodbye (sadly).

If I want to continously make updates on my website, will it cost extra?

No, maintenance is included in our packages with the exception of the starter package where a small fee of R70 extra needs to payed monthly for additional maintenance.
