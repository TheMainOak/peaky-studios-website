About Peaky Studios

We are young entrepreneurs driven by an appetite for success and fuelled by the unrealized potential of Africa. We prioritize not only our personal success but the success of South Africa and Africa as a whole. It is not by chance that foreign global investors are always keeping a close eye on Africa. They realize our potential as a continent and we, as a people, need to realize it too. Hence, here at Peaky Studios we believe internal growth and investment is far more robust and sustainable than external investments. We want to play an instrumental part in stimulating South Africa’s stagnant economy as well as Africa’s. We believe one of the most effective ways to do so is by inspiring young entrepreneurs to play a role through innovation and furthermore, accelerate the growth of small to medium businesses by turning good organisations into great ones through creative digital solutions. We are proud to provide services and tools to achieve this vision for the future. The future is now.
